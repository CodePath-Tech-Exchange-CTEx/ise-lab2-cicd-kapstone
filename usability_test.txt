# Usability Test

## Intro
Hi, my name is Johnny and we are a team of 4 students building a fitness social media app called SDS. Today we'd like you to help us test two new features we're designing. You will be playing the role of a fitness enthusiast who uses the app regularly.

Your job is not to impress us — there are no right or wrong answers. We want to see how you naturally interact with the design. Please think out loud as much as possible — tell us what you're looking at, what you're thinking, and what you expect to happen before you do it.

We will be showing you paper screens and you can point or "tap" on them to interact. We'll swap screens based on your actions.

---

## Tasks

### Feature 1: Meal Plan

Scenario: You just finished a workout and you want to plan out your meals for the week to help you hit your fitness goals. Please show us how you would do that using this app.

### Feature 2: Goal Setter

Scenario: You want to set a new fitness goal for yourself and track your progress toward it. Please show us how you would do that using this app.
